why solar?

Solar Energy, Clean Energy for a better tomorrow.

Help save the world

The environmental impact of non-renewable energy is endangering to the living health on the planet. Lucky for humankind, every hour the sun beams onto Earth enough energy to satisfy global energy needs for an entire year*. The solution is right in front of our eyes and with advanced technologies its now more affordable and efficient to harvest solar power.

How does it affect me?

Investing in solar power today will lock your energy costs for years to come. You can save a major chunk on your energy bills and even feed back the excess to use it on the days with low solar supply. Make the most out of government benefits in the form of subsidies and tax benefits when you invest in solar power and add more to your annual savings.

What can you do as a corporate?

As a corporate, you have an added responsibility towards the environment, you can help reduce you carbon footprints by making the switch and making your office spaces Zero Energy buildings. You can also add more stars to your Corporate social responsibility initiatives when you invest in solar power and help bring many household a sustainable form of energy.
